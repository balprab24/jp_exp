FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Have a question? Give us a call

What are your store hours?

We're open every single day, 9:30 AM to 9:30 PM. No holidays off, no weekend weirdness — same hours, 7 days a week.

Do you offer delivery?

We're in-store pickup only for now. Swing by 6859 N Teutonia Ave — it's quick, we know the regulars by name, and you walk out with exactly what you came for.

Can I place a special order for a product you don't carry?

Absolutely. Call us at (414) 555-0199 with the product details and we'll try to bring it in. Most special orders land within a week.

Do you price match with other stores?

Yes — bring in a printed or in-app price from any local Milwaukee competitor on the exact same product and size, and we'll match it at the register.

What payment methods do you accept?

Cash, debit, all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover), plus Apple Pay and Google Pay. Minimum $10 on card purchases.
